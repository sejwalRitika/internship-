ExlCareer Internship Program

Welcome to ExlCareer internship program!

Our program offers opportunities for students to gain valuable work experience and learn from industry professionals in various fields. As an intern, you will have the chance to work on real-world projects, develop your skills, and contribute to the growth of our organization.

We offer internships in the following areas:

  • Business Development Intern
  • Operations Intern
  • Research Intern
  • Human Resource Intern
  • Copywriting Intern
  • Marketing and Branding Intern
  • Social Media Intern
  • Web Development Intern
  • Graphic Designing Intern
  • Seo intern
  • Campus Ambassador

    • Our program provides hands-on training, mentorship, and networking opportunities. We believe in providing our interns with a comprehensive learning experience that prepares them for their future careers. Interns will have the opportunity to showcase their skill. and talents and make meaningful contributions to our organization. We invite you to explore our internship opportunities and submit your application. Please indicate which internship you are interested in and provide your resume and a cover letter explaining your qualifications and why you are interested in joining our team.

    Business Development Intern

    We are looking for a motivated Business Development intern to assist in expanding our business. The ideal candidate will be a self-starter with excellent communication skills and the ability to work independently. As a Business Development intern, you will be responsible for identifying potential clients, developing business proposals, and participating in sales meetings. This is a great opportunity to gain hands-on experience in business development and to learn from industry professionals.

    Roles and Responsibilities -

  • Research and identify potential clients and partnerships
  • Develop and execute strategies to acquire new business and increase revenue
  • Build and maintain relationships with existing clients and partners
  • Participate in meetings and presentations with clients and partners
  • Conduct market research and analyze trends in the industry

    • SEO Intern

    We are seeking a motivated and enthusiastic SEO Intern to join our team. This internship is an excellent opportunity for individuals interested in building a career in digital marketing and SEO.​

    Roles and Responsibilities -

  • Monitor website analytics using tools like Google Analytics and generate reports on website performance.
  • Assist in off-page optimization efforts, such as link building and outreach campaigns.
